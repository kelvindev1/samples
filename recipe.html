RECIPE FOR CHICKEN FRY'S

INGREDIENTS

  • 500 grams Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • 2 tbsp Kashmiri Chilli Powder
  • 2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Garam Masala Powder
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  • 4 tbsp Lemon Juice or Vinegar
  • 4 tbsp All Purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp Rice Flour
  • Red Food Colour (optional)
  • Oil for deep frying

CHICKEN FRY (STEP-WISE PICTURES)

  1. Take garlic, ginger and curry leaves in a blender
  2. Grind to a coarse puree without adding water




  3. Take chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, pepper powder, garam masala powder, turmeric powder and salt in a bowl



  4. Add the ground garlic and curry leaves puree


  5. Add lemon juice or vinegar


  6. Add all purpose flour / maida


  7. Add in rice flour


  8. Mix well


  9. Add in water


  10. Make it into a thick paste


  11. Add in chicken cubes. this is boneless chicken thighs


  12. Mix well


  13. Add some red food colour. This step is optional


  14. Mix well. leave this to marinate for 1 to 2 hours


  15. Deep fry in hot oil


  16. Cook till golden brown.


  17. Drain them


  18. Serve
